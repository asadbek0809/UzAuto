WARRANTY PERIOD:

The warranty applies to cars sold through authorized dealers, and for cars used under normal conditions, which is 36 months from the date of acceptance by the car owner, or a total of 100,000 km, depending on which one occurred earlier.

The warranty applies to a particular vehicle and does not depend on the owner. When selling a car, the seller must hand over the service book to the new owner and put the seal of the authorized dealer, as well as enter the information of the new owner in the service book. During the warranty period, the new owner of the car will have the same rights to the warranty, provided that all the requirements of the manufacturer specified in the service manual are met.
